Nova Smash

System Design

Nova Smash was a Pinball inspired physics game with character collection elements. At Playnext I took on the role of System Designer and was responsible for all the numbers and balancing for the project. Additionally I was heavily involved in all character and ability design as well as implementation of all unit creation. I worked closely with the Engineering and QA teams to ensure the mechanics were behaving in the intended manner.
